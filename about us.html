The Mandate

Our Mandate for ministry was received from the LORD Himself, in an 18 hour vision. During this vision, a commission was received from the LORD to liberate mankind in all facets of human existence, to restore broken destinies, to bring healing to the infirmed. This was the Divine mandate received from the LORD: Now the hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the word of faith, and I am sending you to undertake this task Today, testimonies of liberation through our messages, books, tapes, magazines and other periodicals are most humbling. The word of faith is working like fire for the liberation of mankind across the nations.

About us

Living Faith Church, Irewon aka Winners' Chapel International, was established on Sunday 18th December, 2011, with Pastor Ayanbadejo as the acting Resident pastor. Just like a mustard seed, the church has been experiencing tremendous growth, all the glory of God. The rented old facility could not accommodate the growing attendance of worshippers, hence the need to acquire her own property. July 2017 four plots of land was acquired very close to the oid facility. Construction work began in September 2020 after the lockdown and on Saturday 5, December 2020, the new sanctuary was dedicated to the glory of God. The facility was completed within a record time of 3 months and 2 weeks and Sunday 6th December 2020 was first Sunday service and on Tuesday 8 December 2020 was our first Shiloh tagged Shiloh 2020: Turn Around Encounters.
